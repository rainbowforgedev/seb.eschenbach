We all love to detox after a long cold winter and the best way to do so is to start with your liver. this salad has three ingredient that were created by nature to support your liver, detoxify it and make it work smoothly.

If you manage to include this salad as your lunch you will see the difference in your mood and energy.

Comments of how you feel after a week liver detox salad for lunch are welcome!

- 5 large shaved asparagus
- 1/2 a bunch chopped edible chrysanthemum
- 1 cup chopped dandelion greens
- 2 tablespoons olive
- 2 tablespoons raw apple cider vinager
- salt and pepper

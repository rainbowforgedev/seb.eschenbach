**The Way of Nature - From Spring equinox to fall Equinox 2025**

Hi Fam! We been dreaming of a cohort of self minded people ready to share collective healing and create stronger bonds as we see clarity in the path we are here to fulfill.  Self driven healers, wellness practitioners, farmers, activist, leaders and organizers ready to welcome abundance, joy and healthy ways of relating to the self and others. 

For the past few years things have been messy yet beautiful in so many ways and as we get to compost everything that we have unlearned, we wanted to create a six month program to hold us accountable and guide us through this time. A program in hopes to deepen our connection to the spirit world, guidance from the divine and collective support and relationship from the nature that we encounter and that each one has to offer.

Our goal is to get to know the power within by getting to know the power of nature; its messages and teachings, collective fortitude to become the best ally to create harmony in this times of chaos  

Some of the feeling that we can work on together: 

- Although I have been successful in my career and have helped so many people,  i still find myself falling into unhealthy patterns and sabotaging true harmony and wholeness within myself.
- I’m ready to drop deeper into my practice, create clear channels so I can support and empower others, Through the knowledge of the earth and everything that has to teach me. 
- I find myself disconnected from the nature inside of me; creating anxiety, depression and low energy when I'm trying to do my work and help others. Would love to get back to the earth’s teachings and learn how i can be a better ally to this planet and people 
In this six months we will listen, connect, learn, breath, make ritual, pray, play and communicate with everything that is. We will learn closely from the elements, plants, guides, angelic beings, creation, ancestors, the self and understand deeply who we are through them. 
I hope you join us in this journey of collective guidance and connection! For more information please contact Suanny.
